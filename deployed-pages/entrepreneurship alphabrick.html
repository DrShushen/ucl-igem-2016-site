Alphabrick

We created this gene circuit using AlphaBrick. A software platform created by 2 of our Igem members as part of the winning team in the UK’s first every synthetic biology biohackathon. The platform makes synbio more accessibly by simplifying the plasmid design process and integrating with transcriptic’s robotic labs to automate the creation of the plasmid. We collaborated with 69 Igem teams, giving them free access to the alpha version for use in their projects.
